When/How are the arxiv papers updated?

A: The updates to the arxiv papers are directly synchronized with the official arxiv website, and the normal delay does not exceed 10 minutes. Arxiv usually updates around 10 a.m. on weekdays (Beijing time), but there may be fluctuations of a few hours. Additionally, updates are not made on Saturdays, Sundays, and some U.S. holidays. If you notice that the arxiv papers on the website have not been updated for an extended period, you can check the update status on the official arxiv website.

Reference: https://info.arxiv.org/help/availability.html#on-deferred-mailings
